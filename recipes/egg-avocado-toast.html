Egg & Avocado Toast

  • Makes 1 serving
  • Preparation time: 10 minutes
  • Ready in 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 slices regular bread
  • 60g avocado
  • 90g sliced tomato
  • 40g chopped onion
  • 40g chopped tomato
  • 180g egg whites
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. In medium bowl, mash up avocado making it into a paste. Add the chopped tomato, paprika, minced garlic, lemon juice, and salt&pepper, and mix.
  2. Place the bread slices in the toaster.
  3. Heat a pan over medium-high heat. Spray with cooking spray for 1 second. Cook the egg whites in the pan. Remove from the pan and divide the cooked egg whites on the open-face toast.
  4. Remove the toast from the toaster and place on a plate. Divide the avocado mash and place on both slices, adding sliced tomato on top.
  5. Serve as two open-face pieces of toast. Serve with fresh-squeezed lemon and cracked pepper if desired.
